Bodybuilding

Bodybuilding is the use of progressive resistance exercise to control and develop one's musculature for aesthetic purposes. An individual who engages in this activity is referred to as a bodybuilder.

Common goals of bodybuilders include building muscle mass, achieving a lean physique, and improving overall strength and endurance. Bodybuilding involves a combination of weightlifting, nutrition, and often supplementation.

For more information and resources on bodybuilding, you can visit reputable fitness and bodybuilding websites or consult with fitness professionals.
